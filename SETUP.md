# Getting Started with PubMetric CRM

Welcome! PubMetric is designed to be a simple, private, and powerful way to track your leads. This guide will help you get set up in just a few minutes.

## 1. Prerequisites

Before you start, you'll need an **Anthropic API Key**. This allows PubMetric to use "Claude" (an advanced AI) to help you draft follow-up emails and summarize your notes.

### How to get an Anthropic API Key:
1. Go to the [Anthropic Console](https://console.anthropic.com/).
2. Create an account (or sign in).
3. Navigate to the **API Keys** section.
4. Click **Create Key**.
5. Give it a name (like "PubMetric") and copy the key. **Keep it safe!**
6. *Note: You may need to add a small amount of credit (e.g., $5) to your account to activate the key.*

## 2. First-Run Setup

When you open PubMetric for the first time, you'll be greeted by a **Setup Wizard**.

1. **Paste your API Key:** Paste the key you copied from Anthropic into the field provided.
2. **Click "Connect & Continue":** PubMetric will verify the key and set up your local database.
3. **Finish:** Click "Go to Dashboard" to start managing your leads.

## 3. Core Features

### Adding Leads
Click the **"Add Lead"** button in the top right. You can enter their name, email, phone number, and any initial notes.

### Tracking Status
Leads are organized by their status:
- **New:** Just added.
- **Contacted:** You've reached out.
- **Interested:** They've shown interest.
- **Converted:** Success! They are now a client.
- **Lost:** They decided not to move forward.

### Using AI Features
- **Draft Follow-up:** Click the "Draft" button on any lead card. Claude will write a personalized follow-up email based on your notes.
- **Summarize Notes:** If you have long notes for a lead, click "Summarize" to get a quick bulleted overview.

## 4. Privacy & Security

- **Local Storage:** All your lead data and notes are stored directly on your computer. We never see your data.
- **Direct Connection:** When you use AI features, PubMetric connects directly to Anthropic. Your data does not pass through our servers.
- **Encrypted Key:** Your API key is stored securely on your device.

## 5. Troubleshooting

- **AI not working?** Ensure your Anthropic account has enough credits and your API key is correct. You can update your key in the **Settings** view.
- **App feels slow?** Since PubMetric runs locally, ensure your computer has enough free memory.
